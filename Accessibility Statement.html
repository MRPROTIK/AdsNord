Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: February 2026

Statement of Commitment

AdsNord is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We aim to provide an inclusive experience for all visitors and continually improve our website and content based on recognized accessibility standards and user feedback.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. AdsNord strives to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA.

Current status: AdsNord is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partial conformance means that some content may not fully meet the standard yet.

Measures We Take to Support Accessibility

We take the following measures to improve accessibility and maintain progress:

  • Inclusive design practices: We consider accessibility during layout, typography, and interaction design.
  • Keyboard navigation support: We aim to ensure core website functions are usable without a mouse.
  • Readable content: We use clear language, consistent headings, and spacing for better scanning and comprehension.
  • Color contrast and focus visibility: We maintain visible focus indicators and avoid using color alone to convey information.
  • Ongoing improvements: We address reported barriers and prioritize fixes that impact core user journeys.

Assessment Approach

We evaluate accessibility using a combination of methods, such as:

  • Self-evaluation: Reviewing pages against WCAG 2.1 AA success criteria.
  • Automated checks: Using accessibility testing tools to detect common issues (e.g., missing labels, contrast risks).
  • Manual testing: Keyboard-only navigation checks and screen reader spot checks on key pages.
Note: Automated tests do not catch everything. Manual testing and user feedback are essential parts of our process.

Known Limitations

While we work toward full WCAG 2.1 AA conformance, some areas may still present barriers, including:

  • Legacy PDFs: Some older PDF audits or downloadable materials may not be fully accessible.
  • Older media: Some older videos may not have captions or full transcripts.
  • Third-party embeds: Certain third-party widgets or embedded content may not fully meet accessibility standards.

If you need an alternative format or assistance accessing content, please contact us and we’ll do our best to help.

Compatibility

Accessibility of AdsNord relies on the following technologies and is designed to work with modern browsers and assistive technologies:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Best results are typically achieved using the latest versions of major browsers and updated assistive technologies.

Feedback & Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of AdsNord. If you encounter accessibility barriers, tell us what page you were on and what you were trying to do—we’ll use that to investigate and improve.

  • Email: info@adsnord.com
  • Address: Nyckelpigevägen 162B, 54965 Skövde, Sweden

We aim to respond within 5 business days.

Escalation

If you contact us and are not satisfied with our response, you may request escalation and we will review your case internally. We will also provide alternative ways to access information when feasible.

Tip: Include your device, browser, and any assistive technology you use (if applicable). This helps us reproduce and resolve the issue faster.
