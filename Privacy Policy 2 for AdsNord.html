4. Data Retention Periods

We do not hold onto data longer than legally required:

  • Inquiry Data: Deleted after 12 months if no contract is signed.
  • Client Data: Retained for 7 years following the Swedish Accounting Act (Bokföringslagen).
  • Analytics Data: Set to expire after 14 months (GA4 standard).
International data transfers

Some of our service providers (such as Google and Meta) may process data outside the EU/EEA. Where international transfers occur, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and other legally recognized mechanisms (including adequacy decisions where applicable).
